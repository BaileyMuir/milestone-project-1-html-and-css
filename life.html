Life of William Shakespeare

1564

During the year 1564, William was born, the third child of Mary and John, but the two older sisters died sometime during their infancy. His date of birth is highly speculated but its believed to be the 23rd of April, three days prior to his baptism at the Holy Trinity Church, recorded in the parish register.

1571

At the age of 7, he would have been enrolled into the king,s new school located on church street, only a short distance from his home on Henley Street.

1577

Due to unforeseeable circumstances to do with his father's failing business, Took William out of school at 13. It speculated to help his father, but the real reason is unknown.

1682

Between now and 1577, not much is said about William, which will be a regular occurrence until he appears in London.

During November of this vary year, aged 18, he would be wed to Anne Hatheway, age 26, in the holy trinity church. At the time, due to pregnancy, she said William was responsible.

1583

A short while after his wedding, his wife would give birth to Susanna on the 26th of May, 1583. His daughter was baptised on the same day as her birth.

1585

Twin children Hamnet and Judith were born on the 2nd of February and, like their sister, baptised on the same day.

1583-1594

Not much is known during this time, but it's believed he had joined a travelling troupe of actors and moved to London.

1594

During this year, he brought shares in the lord chamberlain's men a troupe of actors, and this is when we would start to hear more about his accomplishments.

1596

The tragic news of his son Hamnet death at the age of 11 reached him, and during this time, he returned home, most likely for his sons funeral.

1597

He would purchase New place, the most prominent house in Stratford, displaying his varied wealth.

1601

Tragedy would once again hit Shakespeare with the death of his father.

1603

Due to the death of Queen Elizabeth and the announcement of James 1st, his troupe became the king's men in honour of their new monarch.

1607

His oldest daughter married an excellent dr, john hall, on the 5th of June 1607.

1608

Tragedy would beset Shakespeare yet again with the death of his mother, but it was not all bad; that same year, his first and only grandchild would be born to his oldest daughter on the 21st of February 1608, baptised the same day.

1613

On the 29th of June 1613, due to cannon fire during a play, the globe theatre burning it down.

1616

On the 23rd of April, William had died and would later be buried in the sanctuary of the parish church.
