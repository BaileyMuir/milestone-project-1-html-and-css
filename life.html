Life of William Shakespeare

1564

during the year of 1564 William was born, the third child of mary and john but due to unknown circumstances the two older sisters died sometime during there infancy.

his date of birth is highly speculated but its belived to be the 23rd of April three days prior to his baptism at the Holy Trinity Church which was recorded in the parish register

1571

at the age of 7 he would have been enrolled into the king,s new school located on church street only a short distance from his home on Henley street

1577

due to unforciable circumstances to do with his fathers failing business william was taken out of school at the age of 13, its speculated to help his father but the real reason is unknown.

1682

between now and 1577 not much is said about William which will be a regular occurance intill he apears in London.

during november of this vary year aged 18 he would be wed to Anne Hatheway age 26 in the holy trinity church, at the time due to a pregnancy she said william was responsible for.

1583

a short while after his wedding his wife would give birth to his first child susanna on the 26th may 1583, his daughter was then baptised on the same day as her birth.

1585

Both his twin children Hanmet and Judith were born on the 2nd of febuary and like there sister baptised on the same day.

1583-1594

not much is recorded during this time but its believed he had joined a traviling troupe of actors and moved to london.

1594

during this year he brought shares in the Lord chamberlain's men a troupe of actors and this is when we would start to hear more about his accomplishments.

1596

the tragic news of his son Hamnet death at the aged 11 reached him and during this time he retured home mostlikely for his sons funeral.

1597

he would purchase new place which was the largest house in stratford at the time at the time would be a display of his vary wealth.

1601

tragidy would once again hit shakespeare with the death of his father.

1603

due to the death of queen elizebeth and the annointment of jaimes 1st his troupe bacame the king's men in honer of their new monarch.

1607

his oldest daughter married to a respectable dr john hall on 5th june 1607.

1608

tragidy would beset shakespeare yet again with the death of his mother but it was not all bad that same year his first and only grandchild would be born to his oldest daughter on the 21st of febuary 1608 and baptised the same day.

1613
